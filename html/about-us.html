Why Amalga Exists

The BPO Industry Has a Problem

Somewhere along the way, the BPO industry forgot it's a people business. It got really good at filling seats, and really bad at keeping the people in them.

When Jens Erik Gould was working in the financial sector, he saw the same pattern: operational bottlenecks, high turnover, and difficulty scaling reliable teams. He founded Amalga in 2021 to fix that.
